# Diffusion Notes

Open study notes and lab logs from Week 1 of my [diffusion engineering sprint](https://github.com/itonskie/diffusion-sprint). Coming from an LLM-and-agents AI engineering background, learning image/video diffusion in public.

Not polished. Not authoritative. These are working notes — what surprised me, what tripped me up, what I had to look up twice.

## Structure

| Dir | What's in it |
|-----|--------------|
| `theory/` | Notes on diffusion fundamentals, key papers, architectural concepts |
| `lab-log/` | Daily log of training runs, what worked, what broke, costs |
| `workflows/` | Screenshots and notes from ComfyUI workflows I've explored |
| `configs/` | AI-Toolkit / Musubi Tuner configs that successfully trained, with annotations |

## Reading order

Start with `theory/00-index.md` for a guided path through the fundamentals.
